The Demo


First, the homepage

Assortment of fully grown fruit trees for the user to choose from


Second select the tree

When the mouse hovers over the image it will enlargen slightly


Getting into the tree transformation!

Depending on the fruit tree, the window will open a new page where you can care for the tree. The tree begins as a baby and must be watered or fertilized!


Water the Tree!

When you water the tree, it will blossom!


Fertilize the Tree!

When you fetilize the tree, it will mature by adding more fruit or changing color. You can reload the page to start again or hit the back button to choose another tree!
